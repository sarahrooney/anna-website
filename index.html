about me

I make work work for the Internet. At school they called me a new media artist, on YouTube they call me a creator, on Instagram they call me an influencer, and in the advertising world they call me a strategist. I’ve gained an audience of over 200,000 across online platforms where I create video, photography, text and code to share vlogs, tutorials, internet and social media art that encourages other young women to be critical of and take part in shaping the world around us.

My work is heavily informed by my online community of young girls and their feedback, as well as my background in fine art photography, video and new media—having received my BFA from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in 2014. My passion for communicating in contemporary ways

brought me into the advertising world with Havas Chicago, where I started working as an intern in 2014, and now work as a Senior Strategist across multiple brands.

My brand experience at Havas includes Reynolds Consumer Products, AutoZone, Ragú, Bertolli, and Kmart. In addition to my agency work, I’ve worked as a social influencer for the past five years and have worked with brands such as e.l.f. Cosmetics, Graze, and SEE Eyewear to create lifestyle content. I offer consulting and strategic guidance as services, and have helped start-ups and brands navigate their presence online. I enjoy public speaking and have been invited to speak at conferences, universities, and organizations in the US and abroad.

CONSULTING

SOCIAL MEDIA AUDIT:

If social media was easy, everyone would have a kick ass social media presence. I’m here to make it easier for you. I’ll start with a thorough analysis of your social channels, evaluating everything from what’s working with your audience to the overall cohesiveness of your presence. After I assess, I’ll identify 3 main areas of opportunity so your brand can really flourish.

INFLUENCER STRATEGY:

Partnering with a trusted voice within a community online can be a great strategy for a brand. As someone who works actively in both the influencer world as well as the agency world, I’ve developed a unique, nuanced perspective on brands can best collaborate with influencers in a way that’s rewarding for all parties. I will help identify if an influencer makes sense for your brand’s goals, and develop a strategy ranging from long-term partnerships to short-term reach-driven campaigns.

COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT / CUSTOMER CARE STRATEGY:

Not only do they serve as the public-facing voice of your accounts, community managers create authentic 1:1 engagements with a community, deliver insights around content performance, and drive business objectives on the daily.

SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY:

A full-fledged social media strategy starts out with my social media audit. I’ll create a social strategy for your brand that aligns with your business objectives, identifying areas of opportunity to pursue your goals, what channels make the most sense, and how to activate your intended audience.
